About Us

Cafe Bombay a family restaurant that serves Authentic Traditional Indian Food. Indian cuisine plays an important role in the Indian culture and is known for its sophisticated and subtle use of many spices and herbs. The Tandoori food is one of the types of Indian cuisine. Its name comes from the Tandoor that is a cylindrical clay oven used in India and nearby countries for cooking and baking.

We invite you to taste centuries of tradition and culture in each of our many dishes. Even if it’s your first try of Indian cuisine there will be something of your liking from mild flavors to spicy, Tandoori Chicken, Chicken Tikka, and many more dishes! Dishes always prepared with fresh ingredients and dedication. Be a part of our family!

Fresh ingredients, layers of delicate flavours and the time-honoured methods of preparing these dishes create healthy meals that nourish your imagination while satisfying your appetite. The subtle aroma of tandooris, dals, yakhni, select vegetarian dishes, and other Indian Specialties beckon passes by to stop. This restaurant is inviting and the food is delicious.
